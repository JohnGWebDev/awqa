Cloud Based Storage

Access your aquarium data anytime, anywhere. Our secure cloud-based storage ensures your water quality logs, history, and insights are always backed up and synced across all your devices. Stay connected to your aquariums, whether you're at home or on the go!

Multi-Aquarium Management

Manage water quality for all your aquariums in one convenient place. Each tank gets its own dedicated logbook to help you stay organized. Start with up to three aquariums for free, with the option to add more as your collection grows!

Features under Development

We're excited to share the upcoming tools we have under development, suggested by the community:

  • Log Entry Archival
    Easily organize and archive older log entries while keeping your current data front and center. Access historical logs whenever you need them.

    Stay on top of every detail with the ability to log events like adding livestock, planting new flora, feedings, water changes, and more.

    Manage your aquatic plants with ease. Monitor their growth, maintenance needs, and compatibility with your tank's environment.

    Never miss a critical task again. Set custom alerts for feedings, water changes, equipment maintenance, and more.
